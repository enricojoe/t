ICoSITeR 2021


International Conference on Science, Infrastructure Technology and Regional Development (ICoSITeR) is an annual international conference 2020 which is held by the Institut Teknologi Sumatera (ITERA). This year, ICoSITeR 2020 presents a theme: “Industry 4.0-based Science and Technology for National Independence and Advancement”. ICoSITeR 2020 is expected to connect between companies, organizations, stakeholders, and academia who are active in the fields of technology and infrastructure. ICoSITeR 2020 is expected also to be a place for knowledge exchanges, breakthroughs, and ideas related to the development of various fields in an effort to increase the nation’s competitiveness on a global level.
